About our farm

a few words about DonPablo

Our little coffee farm is located in the township of San Sebastián de Palmitas Medellin, where with effort and dedication, it has been able to adapt to the cultivation of great quality and flavor, making our visitors have a wonderful experience.

During the tour you will understand the effort and dedication necessary to get to our table a cup of the best coffee in the world and in this process you will enrich yourself with new experiences that will bring new nuances to your palate.
